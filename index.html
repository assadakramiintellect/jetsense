A Prescription
for Clarity

Medications can often be confusing. Especially when they're accompanied by pages of instruc-tions. So, we took to Facebook Messenger to assist patients of Plutona - a disposable injection pen. PlutoBot let's patients interact directly with the manufacturer for clarity on ingredients, interactions, and more.

Want to watch how to properly administer the medication? Curious about temperature require-ments? PlutoBot makes the process less stressful for patients, both new and old.

Results? we have those too!

I accidentally left my Plutona prescription out of the fridge for a day. Can I put it back in?

Thanks for visiting Plutona's PharmaChat. Once you take Plutona out of the fridge for an extended period of time, you should not put it back in the refrigerator. You can keep Plutona at room tem-perature (below 77° F) for up to 14 days. If you don't use it within 14 days, you must dispose of it.

I've never injected my Plutona before. The doctor showed me, but I can't remember. How do I use the injection pen?

You should dispose of your Plutona pen in a sharps container - NEVER throw it away in a regular trash or recycling container. Click here to sign up for Plutona Complete's sharps disposal service.
